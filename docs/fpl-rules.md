# Fantasy Premier League Rules
> Sections marked 🅒 are Classic-only. Sections marked 🅳 are Draft-only. Unmarked sections apply to both formats.

## Squad Composition
15 players: 2 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 3 FWD.

## Managing Your Squad
### Starting 11
Select 11 players by the Gameweek deadline. Any formation is valid provided it includes 1 GK, 3+ DEF, and 1+ FWD. Points are scored by these 11; non-playing starters may be auto-substituted.

### Automatic Substitutions
Bench players replace starters who don't play (appearance on pitch, or yellow/red card counts as playing). Substitutions are processed at the end of the Gameweek in the bench order you set:
- GK replaced by backup GK only
- Outfield players replaced by highest-priority bench player who played and doesn't break formation rules

### Defensive Contribution
DEF: **10+ clearances, blocks, interceptions (CBI) and tackles** = 2 pts.
MID/FWD: **12+ CBI, tackles and recoveries** = 2 pts.

### Clean Sheets
Awarded for not conceding while on pitch and playing 60+ minutes (excl. stoppage time). Being substituted before a goal is conceded preserves the clean sheet.

### Red Cards
A red-carded player continues to be penalised for goals conceded. Red card deductions include any yellow card deduction.

### Assists
Awarded to the player who last touches the ball before the goalscorer. The touch can be a pass, inadvertent touch, or shot.

**Passes:** Deflected pass inside the box - assist if recipient scores. Outside the box - only if intended target scores. No assist if 2+ defensive touches, if the defensive touch is a pass, or if the scorer loses and regains possession.

**Touches:** Inadvertent touches count only if they lead directly to the goal scoring opportunity.

**Shots and Rebounds:** A save, block, or woodwork rebound from a shot (not a cross/pass) awards an assist. Shot need not be on target. Deflections before the rebound don't cancel the assist if the shot remains on target. No assist for a second attempt after a blocked/saved shot.

**Own Goals:** Same rules as passes/shots above - the last touch before an own goal earns an assist.

**Penalties and Free-Kicks:** The player who won the penalty/free-kick gets an assist (unless they also score). Shot deflecting to handball = assist if shot was on target; pass/touch deflecting to handball = no assist.

**Finalising:** Assists are provisional until one hour after the final whistle of the last match. Final decisions by FPL in consultation with Stats Perform.

### Bonus Points
Top 3 BPS scorers in each match receive 3, 2, and 1 bonus points respectively. Ties: all tied players receive the same bonus; the next tier is skipped accordingly.

## Deadlines
All team changes must be made by the Gameweek deadline (90 minutes before the first kick-off). Deadlines won't change within 24 hours of the scheduled time.

## Leagues
### League Types
- **Invitational** - create a league, share the code. Cup auto-created. Max 30 private leagues, unlimited teams per league.
- **Public** - randomly assigned teams. Max 5 public leagues.
- **Global** - auto-entered: overall, country, supported team, start-GW, Second Chance (GW21), Fantasy Cup. Qualifying leagues: top 1% and top 10% from 24/25 season.

### League Scoring
**Classic:** Ranked by total points. Tiebreak: fewest transfers (WC/FH transfers excluded). Can join/leave anytime. Scored in monthly phases (Aug-May).

Transfer hits in the GW before a phase starts don't count against the phase score.

**Head-to-Head:** Each team plays one match per GW. Win = 3 pts, draw = 1 pt. Tiebreak: most game points. Fixtures generated at league start; league locks after that. Odd-number leagues get an average-scoring team to balance fixtures.

**H2H Knock-out:** Optional final stage (up to 3 GWs). Average team replaced by next-placed team. Tiebreaks: most goals scored > fewest conceded > coin toss.

## Cups
Knockout competition running alongside leagues. Overall Cup: GW15-GW38. League Cups auto-created for Invitational Classic leagues (start date depends on league size). Fan League and Country Cups also auto-entered. All finals in GW38.

Each round is one GW. Highest GW score (minus transfer hits) progresses. Tiebreaks: most goals scored > fewest conceded > coin toss.

## Classic Format 🅒
### Budget
Initial squad must not exceed **£100m**.

### Players Per Team
Max **3 players** from any single Premier League team.

### Captain & Vice-Captain
Captain's score is doubled. If captain doesn't play, vice-captain becomes captain. If neither plays, no score is doubled.

### Transfers
Unlimited free transfers before your first deadline. After that: 1 free transfer per GW, unused transfers roll over up to a **maximum of 5**. Extra transfers cost **4 points** each. Max 20 transfers per GW (does not apply when playing Wildcard or Free Hit).

### AFCON Transfers
Between GW15 (Sat 6 Dec) and GW16 (Sat 13 Dec), all managers are topped up to 5 free transfers. These can be carried over.

### Player Prices
Prices fluctuate based on transfer market popularity (changes begin when the season starts). Selling price includes a **50% sell-on fee** (rounded up to nearest £0.1m) on any profit.

### Chips
One chip per GW. Each chip is available **twice per season**, split at the GW19 deadline:

| Chip | Effect | Cancellable? |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Bench Boost | Bench players' points count | Yes, before deadline |
| Triple Captain | Captain points tripled | Yes, before deadline |
| Free Hit | Unlimited transfers for one GW; squad reverts next deadline | No, once confirmed |
| Wildcard | All transfers in the GW are free | No, once confirmed |

Free Hit cannot be played in consecutive GWs. Wildcard and Free Hit preserve any banked free transfers.

## Draft Format 🅳
Exclusive ownership - each player belongs to one manager in the league.

### How It Differs from Classic
- **No captains** - all players score at 1x
- **No budget, no player prices**
- **No transfers or hits** - acquisition via waivers and free agents
- **No chips**
- **No 3-per-team limit**

### The Draft
Snake-order: managers pick one player per round until all squads have 15 (2GK/5DEF/5MID/3FWD). Pick order reverses each round.

**Draft order:** League admins can set the draft order using reverse league standings (initial draft uses previous season standings; redrafts use current standings).

**Redrafts:** Admins can schedule up to 3 additional drafts during the season (e.g. around the January window). Redrafts are set up via the League Admin page. Must be scheduled before the prior Gameweek's deadline; once the immediately preceding GW begins, the time is locked.

### Waivers
Primary acquisition method during the season. Submit claims on unowned players each GW, nominating which squad player to drop. Priority starts as reverse draft order; successfully claiming a player drops you to the bottom.

Processed **24 hours before the GW deadline**. After processing, **free agency** opens and runs until the deadline itself.

**Exception:** if the draft completes less than 48 hours before the next deadline, all unselected players go straight to free agency (no waiver round).

**Locked players** (unavailable for waivers): players added to the game within 24 hours of the waiver deadline, or players recently dropped from another squad. Locked players become available at the next waiver deadline.

### Free Agents
Unclaimed players can be picked up first-come, first-served in the **24-hour window before each GW deadline**. Free agent pickups don't affect waiver priority.

### Trades
Leagues can be configured with one of four trade settings (must be set before the draft):
- **No trades** - disabled
- **All trades** - accepted trades processed instantly
- **Administrator approval** - accepted trades can be vetoed by the league admin
- **Manager approval** - accepted trades can be vetoed by other managers; 50% objection kills the trade

Trade offers can be made each GW until the waiver deadline (or 24 hours before it, if approval is required). Trades must be **like-for-like by position** (e.g. 1 MID + 2 FWD for 1 MID + 2 FWD is valid; 2 MID + 1 FWD for 1 MID + 2 FWD is not). Trades are processed before waivers.

### Weekly Management
Lineup, formation, bench order, and auto-subs work identically to Classic - see [Managing Your Squad](#managing-your-squad).
